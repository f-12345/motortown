Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Motor Town Inc ("Motor Town," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect through motortowncars.com (the "Site"), how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

When you use our Contact Us form or otherwise reach out to us, we may collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Any message or details you provide about a vehicle or service request
  • Your text messaging consent preferences (marketing and/or informational)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to inquiries about vehicles, financing, trade-ins, and service
  • Contact you by phone, email, or text message about your inquiry, in line with the consent you provide
  • Send you requested appointment reminders and order/service updates
  • Send marketing communications, but only if you have opted in to marketing text messages or email

SMS / Text Messaging Privacy

No mobile information, including phone numbers and messaging consent, will be shared, sold, or provided to any third party or affiliate for marketing or promotional purposes. This applies to all categories of SMS consent, including marketing and non-marketing/informational messages.

Your mobile opt-in data and consent status are used solely to send you the messages you've agreed to receive from Motor Town Inc. Message and data rates may apply, and message frequency may vary. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, or get help by replying HELP, to any text message from us.

Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information (including PII and messaging consent data) with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the Site or process inquiries (for example, lenders you ask us to submit a credit application to), and only as needed to provide the service you requested, or as required by law.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide to us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message, or by contacting us directly using the information below. You may also ask us to update or delete the personal information we have on file for you.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
